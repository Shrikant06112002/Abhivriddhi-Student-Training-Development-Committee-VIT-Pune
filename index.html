About Us

What is Abhivriddhi ?

Abhivriddhi is the official student training and development committee of VIT Pune. Our vision/resolve is to help students strengthen their academic prowess while assisting them in identifying and pursuing their professional interests and equipping them with the dexterity required for the industrial world.
